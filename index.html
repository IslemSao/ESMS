< What is SOLO BYTE >

Is an exciting coding competition organized by the CSE Club! This unique event challenges participants to solve complex coding problems individually, with no communication allowed between contestants. Whether you're a coding enthusiast or a newcomer, Solo Byte offers a platform to showcase your skills, foster strategic thinking, and compete against top talents.
